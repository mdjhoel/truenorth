About

Stacey MacLean, MA (C. Psyc), LCT

Hello! My name is Stacey and I’ve been a Licensed Counselling Therapist since 2013, with a specialization in trauma. If you’re suffering, I want to help you to suffer less. If you feel lost, I am here to assist and guide you. If you need a hand rebuilding, I’m here to walk beside you.

Our Services

more_vertSexual Trauma Therapy

Mental Health Counselling, specializing in Trauma, Sexual Violence and Indigenous Health.

closeMore on Trauma Therapy

We use a range of therapeutic modalities, including EMDR, Somatic Experiencing, and CBT, to help clients process trauma, reduce symptoms, and develop new coping mechanisms. Our approach is gentle and client-led.

more_vertIndigenous-Focused Counselling

Culturally-sensitive counselling that honors Indigenous ways of knowing and healing.

closeMore on Indigenous Counselling

Our sessions are grounded in respect for cultural traditions and the unique experiences of Indigenous peoples. We work with clients to incorporate traditional healing practices and address the impacts of colonialism and intergenerational trauma.

more_vertIndividual & Group Therapy

We offer both one-on-one sessions and supportive group environments for shared healing.

closeMore on Therapy Options

Individual sessions provide a private setting for deep personal work. Our group therapy offers a safe community space to connect with others who have similar experiences, fostering a sense of belonging and mutual support.
